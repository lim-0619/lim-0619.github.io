I am Li Minghao. I received my M.Eng. in Mechanical Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2024, advised by Professor Xin Li From the State Key Laboratory of Fluid Power and Mechatronic Systems. Prior to my master’s program, I obtained my B.Eng. in Mechatronics Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2021. Currently, I work as an Embedded System Engineer at the Institute of Embedded Computing Platform, Guodian Nanjing Automation Co., Ltd. (SAC). 

My research focuses on the development of special robotic platforms to enable autonomous locomotion and functional execution in complex unstructured terrains, such as rough outdoor environments, net structures and staircases. I had independently developed a rope-climbing mobile robot and contributed to the research of a rope-aided self-traction robot. Currently, my research interests extend to integrating intelligence into both special and general robotic platforms — including humanoid robots and dexterous manipulators, etc. My aspiration is to  translate theoretical algorithms into practical robotic systems that deliver reliable performance and tangible application value in real-world environments. 

Likewise, I believe outstanding robotic research equals outstanding engineering practice. Therefore, I place great importance on the practical application of my research and always maintain enthusiasm for new scenarios and challenges.
